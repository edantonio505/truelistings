All open houses are held at our model townhome on 17 Clermont. This 3,000 square foot townhouse at Navy Green is an expansive and well designed home, boasting four bedrooms, two full and two half bathrooms, a media room, large laundry room, and garden with patio. The floors of each home are finished in 7 wide DuChateau natural wood and is equipped with a video intercom. Each home is individually deeded and owned outright.
